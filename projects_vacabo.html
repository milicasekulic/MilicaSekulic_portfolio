VACABO

Vacabo Travel was founded in 2016. As it continues to grow, Vacabo is looking to leave its mark on the travel industry by offering a variety of products and services. Vacabo is a one stop shop for everyone’s travel needs.

As a newbie in the industry, Vacabos’ blueprint started with the design of its unique logo. Once the logo was designed an exclusive and vibrant color scheme was chosen. Vacabo’s website was built around the unique logo and color scheme. The design is user friendly and inviting to its current and future customers, creating a comfortable experience for everyone.
