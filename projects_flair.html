FLAIR AIRLINES

Flair Airlines Ltd. is a privately owned Canadian charter airline based in Kelowna, B.C. For this company I did a redesign of the logo identity and whole branding guidelines and applications, from aircraft livery, onboard menu, flying magazines and airport print materials to all visuals for social media and marketing ads, both printed and digital.

Redesign of the Flair website was done in 2 phases. The first goal was to make an easier booking process for users, a friendlier interface and more options for users.The second stage of redesign was to concentrate on the mobile approach first, and new type of input features like voice research data.
