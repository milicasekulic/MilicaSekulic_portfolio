DYNAMIC INTERNATIONAL AIRWAYS

Dynamic International Airways is a US Certified PAR Part 121 air carrier. With its fleet of Boeing 767 it strives to provide low cost, long haul, point to point service to its customers. Headquartered in High Point, North Carolina it offers services in New York, Guyana, Venezuela, Ecuador, and Asia. With its addition of bilingual flight attendants (Spanish and English) it has been able to provide higher quality service to its Central and South American markets.

The focus on Dynamic’s recently developed website was on being more user friendly. Creating a more user friendly website enhances the ease of booking a flight along with finding airline policies and questions. This helped in reducing calls with frequently asked questions to the call center. Passengers new and old have raved about the new design and its feature of being more user friendly.
