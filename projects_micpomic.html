MIC PO MIC

micPomic is designed jewelry and also first design work done by me. Guided by the desire to make the jewelry creative and unique, I tried to use different combinations of colors and shapes. I mainly worked with cotton and wool, decorating them with semiprecious stones, chains, wooden and plastic pearls, zircons and other material. micPOmic is intended for every brave women who isn't afraid to show her strong and unique character and style. Jewelry can be ordered via Facebook or purchased at events such as Fashion Move, Mikser Bazaar and Mixer market.
